# Privacy Policy – Water Reminder

Last updated: July 10, 2026

## Overview

Water Reminder ("the app") is built as a free application. This privacy policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details of any kind.

All data generated by your use of the app — including your daily water intake, drink history, daily goal, reminder settings, and theme preference — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## How Your Data Is Used

All data stored by the app is used solely to provide the app's core functionality: tracking your water intake, displaying your progress, and scheduling reminders. No data is used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any other purpose.

## Third-Party Services

Water Reminder does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers. The app does not integrate with any external APIs or services that would result in your data being shared with third parties.

## Notifications

The app may send local push notifications to remind you to drink water. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers. You can enable or disable notifications at any time within the app or through your device's notification settings.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no personal information from any user, it is safe for use by all ages.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. We have no access to your data and therefore cannot delete it on your behalf.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

# 💧 Water Reminder – Daily Hydration Tracker
### Google Play Store Description

---

Stay hydrated, stay healthy. Water Reminder is the simplest and most effective way to track your daily water intake and build a consistent hydration habit that lasts.

Most people don't drink enough water — not because they don't want to, but because they forget. Life gets busy, hours pass, and before you know it you've barely had a glass all day. Water Reminder solves this by making hydration tracking effortless and keeping you accountable with smart reminders throughout your day.

---

🚰 TRACK YOUR WATER INTAKE IN SECONDS

Logging your water has never been easier. With just one tap, you can record 250ml, 500ml, or 750ml of water — covering everything from a small glass to a full bottle. No typing, no fussing, no friction. Just tap and go.

Your daily progress is displayed front and centre with a clean, animated progress bar and a large percentage readout so you always know exactly where you stand. A motivational message updates as you get closer to your goal, giving you that extra nudge to keep going.

---

🎯 SET YOUR OWN DAILY GOAL

Everyone's hydration needs are different. Athletes, office workers, and people in hot climates all need different amounts of water. That's why Water Reminder lets you set your own personal daily goal anywhere between 500ml and 5000ml using a smooth, easy-to-use slider. No more one-size-fits-all targets — your goal, your rules.

Your goal is saved automatically and carried over every day so you never have to set it again.

---

🔔 SMART REMINDERS THAT RESPECT YOUR ROUTINE

Forgetting to drink water is the number one reason people don't hit their daily goal. Water Reminder's smart notification system sends you gentle, timely reminders throughout the day so you never go too long without hydrating.

Choose your reminder interval — every 1, 2, or 3 hours — whatever works for your schedule. Enable or disable reminders with a single toggle. And best of all, once you hit your daily goal, reminders automatically stop. No unnecessary buzzing once you've already crushed it for the day.

Reminders are also automatically restored when you reopen the app, so your schedule stays consistent even after a restart.

---

📋 TODAY'S DRINK LOG

Every time you log water, Water Reminder records the amount and the exact time in your personal drink history. Scroll through your log at any time to see exactly when you drank and how much — a simple, clear record of your day's hydration journey.

Your history is saved locally on your device and carries over between sessions, so you never lose your data.

---

🌙 LIGHT & DARK MODE

Whether you prefer a clean, bright interface during the day or a sleek dark theme at night, Water Reminder has you covered. Switch between light and dark mode with a single tap, and your preference is remembered across every session.

---

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Water Reminder stores all your data directly on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data collection. Your health information stays where it belongs — with you.

💡 DID YOU KNOW?

Staying properly hydrated improves concentration, boosts energy levels, supports healthy digestion, and helps maintain a healthy weight. Even mild dehydration — as little as 1–2% of body weight — can cause fatigue, headaches, and reduced mental performance. Building a consistent water drinking habit is one of the simplest things you can do for your overall health.

Water Reminder makes that habit easy to build and easy to keep.
